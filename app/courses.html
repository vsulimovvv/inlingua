inlingua approach

  • Analysis

    Multiple language fluency is a huge asset for any business or team. At inlingua we make sure to undertake an in-depth analysis to determine your concrete objectives. This is done by collecting your precise needs and setting up a complete and reliable initial evaluation, both written and oral. The information collected allows for the recommendation of a tailor-made program that will fit your needs and lead to your goals.

  • Implementation

    After taking all parameters into account, the chosen training plan is implemented inlingua takes care of the material and the practical organisation of the courses.

  • Flexibility

    The flexibility of the program during the training is ensured through the permanent control of the coordinator, this allows for smooth transitions during the training programme. In order to maximize the efficiency of our language training courses, we make provision for all training to be available either at our language center or on your company’s premises.

Your advantages

Der wahre Schlussel zu unserem Erfolg

  • Support:

    By joining an inlingua language training programme you will have access to our Virtual Classroom or VC sessions from various locations simultaneously via inlingua’s exclusive online classroom platform, my.inlingua.com which offers your team:

    • Personalized learning paths leading to progressive learning success.
    • Additional exercises to improve grammar, vocabulary and pronunciation.
    • Integrated gamification features which increase study motivation.
    • A dictionary and a speaking lab are available only for students learning English.
  • Access:

    Our pedagogical, administrative and accounting teams support your HR and your team throughout the training process and beyond. Being dedicated to each customer and using our extensive knowledge of internal company processes are our specialty.

  • Practical Skills:

    The inlingua method focuses as much as possible on developing practical communication skills in the language of choice, thereby ensuring that you will be able to practically apply your language skills in everyday work situations.

  • Industry Specific Vocabulary:

    Our trainers will specially incorporate terms, phrases and vocabulary that is relevant to your industry into your course programme, thus keeping your training programme as relevant and specific as possible.

Quality Management

At inlingua, maintaining the quality of all our language training programmes is of the utmost importance, we therefore make sure that both the training programme participants as well as the training managers guide the training through their regular input and feedback.

Evaluations are carried out regularly during training in order to monitor and ensure that the programme is goal-driven.

We keep constant communication with all our trainers, course participants and your company to allow for a dynamic offer as well as a fast and where necessary, efficient adaptation.

A final evaluation is made in order to measure progress at the end of the training. After which a detailed progress report is issued, accompanied by advice and recommendations for the next steps.
